Freedom Guard

Form your own guard, and become knowledgeable in security, self defense, wilderness survival, emergency preparedness, first aid, food acquisition/skills (like trapping), shelter construction, and more.

Get experience at training lands.

Goal

To equip individuals with the knowledge they need to maximize their individual freedom/liberty

Freedom guard’s aim and direction of it is to create an intentional focused, coordinated and extremely skilled groups of individuals who can solve problems quickly and efficiently and safely.
