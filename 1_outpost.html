Hello, all who wander! Welcome to the Agorist Welcome Outpost! From here, you can navigate the various areas of our network as you walk through the outpost. Each building or location represents a functioning area in our expanding journey towards freedom. Where would you like to go first?

Intro

We are an outpost of the network and your first Voluntary Welcome Hub.

Welcome Desk

A welcome desk is any informed point of contact or location where you can obtain information on the local network and its affiliates and offerings.


Welcome Desks exist in various forms throughout the network. The criteria for a welcome desk is that it is manned by a network member in some way at least 5 days a week. This means that the person is available to perform welcome duties: Sharing information on the network, directing peope towards resources on their innterests, and initiating contact with Guilds or Network Hosts on behalf of prospective network members.

Camp Orientation

Begin your journey by choosing your own pathway through the Outpost, tailored to your interests and goals. This orientation guides newcomers in discovering the best route into the network community.

The Compass Star

Explore the core of the network and caravan within The Compass Star, your guide to essential information and connections. Here you’ll find resources, contacts, and support to help you navigate your adventure.

The Library

The Library is a curated repository of writings and resources on agorism, voluntaryism, and stateless society. Dive into essays, guides, and knowledge to deepen your understanding and inspire your path.

The Workshop

The Workshop is a collaborative space for practical skill-building, creative projects, and innovation. Connect with others to share tools, ideas, and hands-on experiences that empower personal and community growth.

The Nexus

The Nexus acts as the network’s central meeting point for announcements, coordination, and updates. Stay informed and connected with the latest news, events, and opportunities across the entire caravan.

The Agora

The Agora is the vibrant marketplace and exchange center of the network. Here, members can trade goods, share services, and participate in a dynamic ecosystem built on trust and voluntary cooperation.
