Security Mesh

You already hold a vital position in the Network's security mesh, even if you're not interested in other, more active areas. This powerful, inclusive concept means that simply by being aware of us, you're contributing.

Your involvement grows with your understanding and practice:

  • Awareness: Just knowing about our Network and its vision is a foundational level of participation.
  • Understanding: Deepening your grasp of voluntaryism strengthens the mesh by increasing the collective intellectual defense against coercion.
  • Practice: Actively living out voluntaryist principles in your daily life reinforces the integrity of the mesh.
  • Belief: Believing in the vision and its eventual success adds to the collective resolve and resilience.
  • Contribution: If you are willing to help resolve a problem without the use of the state, no matter how big or small your contribution, you are actively part of the security mesh.

This mesh is a catch-all, designed to empower everyone. It explains how simply supporting voluntaryism, in whatever way you're comfortable, secures your place within our Network. This creates a powerful sense of importance and belonging, ensuring that everyone feels they have a vital role, even if they don't see a fit in more structured activities like Freedom Guard.
