Ready to Find Collaborators?

Looking for partners, mentors, or community members? Contact us at caravanarchy@protonmail.com with:

  • Collaboration type: Partnership, mentorship, skill exchange, etc.
  • What you're offering: Skills, resources, time, space
  • What you're seeking: Specific skills, commitment level, location needs
  • Time commitment: Short-term project, ongoing, live-in, etc.
  • Location requirements: Remote, specific region, in-person

Successful collaborations are built on clear expectations and shared values. Be specific about what you're looking for!

Tips for Successful Collaborations

  • Be specific: Vague requests get fewer responses. Clearly define what you need and what you offer.
  • Start small: Test compatibility with a short project before committing long-term.
  • Use network trust: Ask for references from other members. Build your own reputation.
  • Document agreements: Even simple written agreements prevent misunderstandings.
  • Communicate often: Over-communication early prevents problems later.
  • Have exit plans: Agree upfront how to part ways if it doesn't work out.
