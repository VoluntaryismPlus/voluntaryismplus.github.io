Step Three: Choosing the Right Person


Here is another unpopular opinion we have:

You can make a promise to be with someone forever and be fully prepared to keep it!

You must try to ascertain before getting into a relationship with someone whether or not they are the right person for you, and don’t make any promises until you know.

This takes time, and we have all been fooled, but we have a method by which you could be more certain:

Good Partner Checklist
  • See if they’re willing to talk about anything you find important
  • See if they are devoted to spending more time with you than is socially acceptable.
  • See if they are willing to change for you based on your needs.
  • See if they follow through with these behaviors over time, and if they keep their promises.

Reflect upon these questions:

  • Are you the biggest event in this person's life, in their opinion?
  • Can you tell them everything?

Take time to know all sides of the person

  • Be in different settings with them
  • See whether they desire another person while waiting for you

Who to enter a relationship with:

Check For Compatibility
  • Overall goals in life
  • Achievements
  • What makes you feel successful
  • Making money
  • Social goals
  • Desires for lifestyle
  • Location
  • Shelter type
  • Cost of living
  • Desired relationship dynamics
  • Workload
  • Chores
  • Time together
  • Conversation amount
  • Physical needs
  • Admiration
Is this person:
  • Someone who you don’t see any deal breaker problems with
  • Someone you don’t have animosity towards
  • Someone whose family dynamic you enjoy
  • Someone who would be a good parent (if you want kids)
  • Someone who pleases you with their social interactions
