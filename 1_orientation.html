Where Are You Now?

Most people entering the network are strong in one or two pathways but weak in others. That's normal. The goal isn't perfection‚Äîit's progress.

Quick self-assessment:

  • Money: What percentage of your income comes from agorist sources?
  • Housing: How much monthly housing cost could you eliminate?
  • Community: Do you have 3+ people you trust in an emergency?
  • Purpose: Does your daily work create more freedom for others?

If you scored low on any pathway, that's where to focus next. If you scored high on all four, you're ready to take the road beyond and start building for others.
