Security Mesh

For those who are not interested currently in other areas of the network, you still occupy a certain position in the security mesh.

Just knowing about us is a level.

Knowing and understanding voluntaryism is another.

You might practice voluntaryism in your life.

You might understand what’s possible in this vision.

You might believe that the vision will come true.

If you are willing to help resolve a problem without the use of the state, no matter how big or small your contribution, you are part of the security mesh.
