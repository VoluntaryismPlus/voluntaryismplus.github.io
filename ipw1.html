The Intentional Partnership

Worksheet One: Am I Ready?

Complete this worksheet especially if you are single.

This worksheet is a deep dive into self-awareness and personal responsibility, which are central themes of our seminar. It’s for the individual to complete on their own.

Examining Your Foundation:

A section for a person to reflect on their own beliefs about love and relationships. For example:

Do I truly believe love is a choice?

Am I willing to make changes to myself to be a better partner?

Am I willing to take responsibility for my own feelings and reactions?

Knowing Your Weaknesses:

A space for honest self-reflection.

What are my personal patterns or potential "downfalls"? (Examples)

What is my plan for working against them?

What are my core needs, and am I aware of them? Circle or write (Examples)

Defining Your Promises:

A section to list your personal "non-negotiables" and a few things you are willing to be flexible on.

Your life goal should reach beyond yourself

If someone says it’s too idealistic:

- What if you had someone in your life who will devote themselves toward manifesting that goal?

- Have they designed their goal to fail, or have they come up with a “bulletproof” or “snowballing” goal?

- How much better would people’s lives be if your goal was achieved?
