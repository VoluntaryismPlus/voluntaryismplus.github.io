Agorist Welcome Outpost

Chart Your Course to Liberty: Explore the Outpost of Self-Sovereignty.

Hello, all who wander! Welcome to the Agorist Welcome Outpost! From here, you can navigate the various areas of our network as you walk through the outpost. Each building or location represents a functioning area in our expanding journey towards freedom. Where would you like to go first?

Intro

Welcome to the Agorist Welcome Outpost. Here, you will find the Library of Anarchy, the Outer Realm Bazaar, the Admissions Desk, and the Voluntary Welcome Desk which is headed by the Caravan Aid Committee.

We are an outpost of the network and your first Voluntary Welcome Hub.

Welcome Desk

A welcome desk is any informed point of contact or location where you can obtain information on the local network and its affiliates and offerings.


Welcome Desks exist in various forms throughout the network. The criteria for a welcome desk is that it is manned by a network member in some way at least 5 days a week. This means that the person is available to perform welcome duties: Sharing information on the network, directing peope towards resources on their innterests, and initiating contact with Guilds or Network Hosts on behalf of prospective network members.
