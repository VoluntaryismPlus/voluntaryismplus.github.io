2. Behaviors We Find Less Important

Review the "Behaviors I Find Less Important or Helpful (from my partner)" from both individual pages.

Review each partner’s suggestions for why the other partner does the mentioned behaviors and gain clarity from the partner whose behavior is being assessed.

3. Behaviors We Feel Misunderstood In

Review the "Behaviors I Feel Misunderstood In" from both individual pages.

Gain clarity on what each partner thinks of the behaviors.

Resolve any additional feeling based on this conversation.


4. Committing to Collaborative Actions

Review the "Behaviors I Believe Would Strengthen Our Bond & Support Our Shared Priorities" from both individual pages.

As a couple, agree on a number of specific, actionable commitments you will both make to strengthen your bond and actively support your shared priorities. These should be things you can start doing regularly.

For each commitment, briefly explain how it aligns with your shared priorities and contributes to a stronger relationship.

Make sure each person feels that all of their priorities are being addressed. Is there an alternative solution that is amenable and meets the same need?

Set a Check-in Date: Agree on a specific date to revisit this worksheet and discuss your progress.
