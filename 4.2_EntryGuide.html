Setup Festivals and Events

Create a yearly festival or a weekly farmers market in your area. Create a full-time support team who wants to bring this about.

Join/Start a Caravan

You can take your career and bring it on the road with you.

Professions that benefit from this: Performers, craftspersons, mechanical engineers, speakers, doctors etc

If you have a dream that is more specific than our caravan, you will probably want to start your own caravan. So you can travel as you would in a more tight knit group.

Travel the Bounty Board

Fill the board with bounties, take bounties on for cash or barter. Add to existing bounties.

Trade Locally

Trading from within the network or as an individual agent, we can provide you with a network of customers for your product and access to other agorist goods. We will buy your product or help you freeze dry it.

Find anarchist clients in your area for yourself and others. Enable local trade as a local (or long distance) courier.

Properties

Develop one or more of the properties and help it bring wealth to yourself and the community.

Housing

Agorist Hosts

Agorists hosts are here to help to transition into a new life.

Live on the Free Lands

Live rent free on lands with others who are also developing the land to be habitable and profitable. Upkeep gardens or other infrastructure. Build cities out of pallets or other found materials. You don’t need much money to survive. You just need water, food, and shelter. This isn’t the ideal existence but it can be a place to start.

Road Life

Live in one of our fleet vehicles as you get a feel for your new life as a nomad. Buy or bring your own vehicle to live in.
