The Intentional Partnership

Worksheet Two: Are They the Right One?

Complete this worksheet especially if you are single.

This worksheet is designed to help a person objectively assess a potential partner based on our criteria. The goal is to see if the other person is also prepared to enter into the "Initial Agreement."

Observing Their Readiness:

A checklist or rating system to assess the other person's behavior. (1-5 rating)

Do they show a willingness to talk about everything that is important to you?

Do they demonstrate a devotion to spending time with you?

Do they follow through on their promises?

Do they show interest in knowing themselves and you better?

Alignment Check:

Questions to compare your goals and desires.

How compatible are our overall goals in life (social goals, financial goals, etc.)?

How do I feel about their family dynamic?

Do I feel admiration for this person?

Potential Red Flags:

A space to list any "dealbreaker problems" they've observed that they can’t see themselves working with.

Why would you be with someone you didn’t trust with your life?

Who didn’t want the same things as you?

Who wouldn’t choose the best for you?

Who fulfills all your needs and who does not act manipulatively in conversation

When you are diligently striving toward your life-goal, does having your beloved in your life move you closer to that goal?
