Wealth Path

Building Prosperity in a Free Economy

Welcome to The Wealth Path, your guide to achieving financial freedom and building genuine prosperity outside the coercive systems of the state. Here, you'll discover actionable strategies to leverage your skills, connect with a vibrant market, and create enduring value within our decentralized network. We empower you to redefine wealth, ensuring it serves your liberty and enriches the communities you participate in.

Wealth-Building Opportunities

1. Cultivate Free Markets

Find others who need to sell their goods and services. Together, follow our guide to organize and manage vibrant, decentralized markets and festivals, creating vital economic hubs free from state interference. Build a dedicated team to facilitate these regular or seasonal events, fostering direct trade and community prosperity.

2. Mobilize Your Enterprise

Take your skills and business on the road, expanding your market reach across diverse communities. Whether joining an existing caravan or launching your own specialized collective, enjoy the freedom of mobile commerce and boundless clientele.

Professions that benefit from mobility:

  • Performers
  • Craftspersons
  • Mechanics
  • Restauranteurs
  • Speakers
  • Educators
  • Body Workers

3. Harness the Bounty Board

Leverage our decentralizedBounty Board to find immediate income opportunities or acquire needed goods and services through direct cash or barter. Post your own requests, take on existing challenges, and participate in a dynamic peer-to-peer economy.

4. Master Local & Distributed Trade

Using recources like the Bounty Board and other social networks to identify a dedicated network of agorist customers for your products and services, fostering resilient local economies. Support and encourage local farms to expand their offerings by connecting them to this market of new customers. Become a crucial link in our decentralized supply chains by facilitating efficient, non-state-controlled courier services for goods.

5. Cultivate Productive Properties

Transform undeveloped or underutilized physical properties into thriving centers of wealth generation and community enrichment. Whether through agriculture, craftsmanship, educational opportunities or resource development, create enduring assets that benefit both yourself and the network.

6. Invest in Skill Mastery & Mentorship

Grow your personal wealth by developing highly sought-after skills, and further amplify your impact by teaching others. Leverage our Guild system and The Workshop to specialize, learn, and offer valuable education that strengthens the entire network's economic capacity.

7. Strategic Resource Stewardship

Identify, acquire, and manage essential resources – from sustainable energy solutions to secure digital infrastructure. Generate wealth by creating efficient systems outside of state taxes and regulations that provide vital services and support the network's self-sufficiency.

8. Pioneer Agorist Innovation

Thinking outside of state restrictions, channel your creativity into developing novel solutions, technologies, or artistic expressions that serve the needs of a free society. Build wealth by pioneering innovations that contribute unique value and expand the possibilities of decentralized living. See Beyond State Solutions for examples and ideas.

Guidance for Your Knowledge Level

Not sure where to begin? We've tailored suggestions to match your current understanding of voluntaryism and agorism.

New to Voluntaryism & Agorism?

Start Here: Begin with foundational knowledge. Explore The Library's Intro to Voluntaryism and Why Agorism Works to grasp the core philosophy. Familiarize yourself with the network's foundational elements in The Compass Star's Our Core Principles and The Network: Structure & Principles.


Recommended Action: Try a small bounty on the Bounty Board or attend a local Agora Event to observe direct trade in action.

Already Familiar with the Philosophy?

Dive Deeper: Advance your understanding with The Library's Voluntaryism Plus and explore the strategic nuances in Agorist Tactics: Strategies for Economic Freedom within The Workshop.


Recommended Action: Consider actively joining a Guild related to your skills or exploring opportunities to contribute to a Caravanarchy collective.

Follow Your Preferred Engagement Style

Your journey is unique. Choose how you prefer to engage with wealth-building opportunities.

Ready for Action?

Direct Engagement: Immediately seek out opportunities on the Bounty Board to earn or trade. Connect with Network Hosts for potential localized projects. Consider joining or forming a Nomadic Market & Caravan to take your skills directly to new customers.

Skill Application: Head to The Workshop to apply your existing skills or learn new ones that have direct market value within the network.

Prefer Deeper Exploration/Reflection?

Study & Strategize: Delve into The Library's "Philosophy Section" for essays on economic freedom and societal structures, such as "Downfall of the Greats." Reflect on personal limitations and opportunities with "Socially-Perpetuated Self-Encagement" in Strategic Lifestyle Planning.

Conceptualize & Plan: Use "Freedom Prompts" to brainstorm your own unique wealth-creation strategies within a free society, or explore "Anarchist Innovation Station" for inspiration on new economic models.
