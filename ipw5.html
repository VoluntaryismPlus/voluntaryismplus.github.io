The Intentional Partnership

Worksheet Five: Understanding Our Priorities

Complete this worksheet especially if you are in a couple.

This worksheet act as an informal prerequisite to “aligning our daily lives”. The concept of the worksheet is identifying priorities and then assessing different behaviors or actions with regard to what priority they are serving and how important they are in the service of that priority.

This will be a worksheet that each person fills out independently, and then on the third page, they will work to create a shared vision for what their priorities are as a couple.

The behaviors discussed on the individual pages fall into different sections: behaviors that each partner finds unimportant or not helpful that the other partner does, a section for behaviors each person feels misunderstood in that they do, and behaviors that each partner thinks would help strengthen the bond and support the couples priorities.

This worksheet directly addresses the core of aligning individual lives within a partnership and provides a structured way to uncover hidden assumptions and intentions.

This worksheet is designed to help you individually clarify your core priorities and then, as a couple, align on your shared vision. A strong, lasting partnership is built on understanding not just what each person does, but why they do it, and how those actions serve their deepest values.
