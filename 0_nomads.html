Living Free Shouldn't Mean Living Alone

The Problem:

  • Limited customer base by location
  • Isolation from like-minded people
  • No backup when things go wrong
  • Gig platforms take huge cuts (20-30%)
  • Hard to find quality trades on the road

The Solution:

  • Bounty Board: Your mobile marketplace
  • Festival Circuit: Meet customers AND friends
  • Mutual Aid Network: Backup across states
  • No middleman: Keep 100% of your earnings
  • Vetted community: People who share your values
